Welcome To Sepia

Originated from Europe, we are now located in a large showroom and warehouse located in Vancouver. We stock a wide range of 19th century and early 20th century French, European and English furniture, as well as antique French clocks and historical mirrors. Sepia is known for its well priced items and also nearly perfect condition. We pride ourselves in being helpful, knowledgeable and honest and many of our customers return again and again. Please keep in mind that the Sepia website provides only a selection of our stock, so please contact us if you are after anything specific.
